Trust & Privacy

Your reports are yours. Full stop.

We built this for professionals whose reputation depends on the accuracy and confidentiality of their work. Here's exactly how we handle your data.

Reports never leave your account

Your generated reports are stored in your account only. We do not access, share, sell, or transmit them to any third party — ever.

Not used to train AI models

Your report data, tree data, and client information are never used to train or improve any AI model — ours or anyone else's.

Ordinance data is verified

Municipal ordinances are sourced directly from official city codes and reviewed manually before being added to the system. We flag outdated code and update promptly.

Cancel anytime, keep your data

No lock-in. If you cancel, you can export all your reports in PDF format. We don't hold your work hostage.

Audit trail on every report

Every report includes a timestamped record of what was AI-generated vs. what you edited. Full transparency for your records.

You are always the author

Reports are certified by you, under your ISA credential. TreeCertify's name never appears on a submitted report — it's your professional document.
